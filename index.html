Sofia's Hair Salon

About Us

Established in the year 2010, Sofia's Hair Salon is a popular salon in Los Angeles, California. From haircut, styling to color and other hair treatments, and spa services, the salon offers numerous services for its clients.

Cuts & Style

Haircut: Includes consultation, shampoo, cut and style. We take into consideration of your lifestyle, face-shape and your commitment to your style.

Color

All color services include a cleanse, condition, scalp massage and blow dry.

Facial Treatments

All facials include steaming, exfoliation, and extraction.

Threading & Waxing

Say “Bye-bye!” to unwanted hair. We offer traditional facial threading and waxing.
